About Qurtuba

Established in 2005, Qurtuba Islamic College has been at the forefront of Islamic education, combining traditional Islamic sciences with contemporary academic disciplines.

Our Mission & Vision

To nurture a generation of Muslims who are firmly grounded in their faith, intellectually competent, morally upright, and capable of contributing positively to society.

We envision an educational institution that serves as a beacon of knowledge, combining the best of Islamic tradition with modern academic excellence.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous academic programs that challenge students to achieve their highest potential.

Islamic Values

Education rooted in Quranic teachings and prophetic traditions.

Community Focus

Developing leaders who serve their communities with compassion.

Global Perspective

Preparing students to engage with diverse cultures and global challenges.
